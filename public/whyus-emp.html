Why Us

Introduction

At EMP Laser, we’re not just another reseller—we’re your expert partner in navigating the rapidly evolving world of metal fabrication technology.

The metal fabrication industry has seen remarkable advancements in recent years. High-end components and cutting-edge technologies that were once exclusive to a select few manufacturers are now widely available at significantly lower costs. This shift has made powerful, precision-driven machinery accessible to businesses of all sizes—if they know where to look.

Our journey began in our own metal fabrication facility, where we built a thriving operation using imported machinery and saved a fortune in the process. However, the road wasn’t without its challenges. From navigating the complexities of importing to overcoming shortcomings in product selection and support, we’ve learned firsthand what it takes to succeed in this space. This hard-earned experience is the foundation of EMP Laser.

A Unique Approach to Quality and Cost Savings

At EMP Laser, we reject the notion that one manufacturer has all the answers. No single factory excels across every category. Instead, we act as free agents, partnering with multiple manufacturers to handpick the best products each has to offer. Our partnerships and independent approach allow us to negotiate unbeatable prices and deliver equipment that reflects our rigorous standards.

By rebranding these standout products under the EMP Laser name, we ensure our customers receive only the most reliable, high-performing machinery—without the inflated costs typically associated with purchasing from factories with a domestic presence or covert resellers.

Comprehensive Solutions for Every Fabrication Need

We don’t stop at providing exceptional machines; we specialize in solving every challenge of the buying process. From consulting and product selection to importing, customs clearance, installation, training, and ongoing maintenance, we make the process seamless and stress-free. Whether you’re purchasing an EMP Laser model or seeking guidance on products we don’t OEM, our expertise ensures you’re supported every step of the way.

Our product lineup spans the full spectrum of metalworking needs. Whether you’re cutting, cleaning, cladding, grinding, bending, welding, or milling, EMP Laser has you covered. Our curated selection includes only the best machinery for these applications, giving you access to world-class tools at unmatched value.
