FAQ

What is the differences between JPT and Raycus?

The major differences between JPT and Raycus has to do with the pulse width and the frequency available for each.

Generally speaking a JPT source will allow more control over the beam and allow for whiter whites on aluminum, darker blacks on steels, and brighter colors on plastics. If the goal is to have brighter colors on polymers or have darker colors on steels than a JPT is well worth the upgrade.

If you simply want to dig into metal with an engrave, or just do some simple names on tools, either is a great option.

What are the advantages of a MOPA laser?

A MOPA (Master Oscillator Power Amplifier) fiber laser has several advantages that make it suitable for various applications compared to other types of lasers. Here are some key advantages:

1.Pulse Duration Control: MOPA lasers offer precise control over pulse duration. This is particularly important in applications where the laser needs to produce pulses of specific lengths. The ability to control pulse duration allows for better customization and optimization of the laser for specific tasks.

2.Peak Power Control: MOPA lasers enable control over the peak power of the laser pulses. This feature is crucial in applications such as material processing, where different materials may require different levels of peak power for optimal results.

3.Flexible Pulse Repetition Rates: MOPA lasers can have flexible pulse repetition rates. This flexibility is advantageous in applications where the laser needs to operate at varying speeds or frequencies.

4.Pulse Energy Scalability:MOPA lasers can be designed to scale pulse energy while maintaining a consistent pulse shape. This scalability is beneficial in applications that require varying levels of energy for different tasks.

What's the difference between the 30W or 50W fiber lasers?

30W is fantastic for most engraving that isn't deep, but anytime you want to go deep or cut thin metals (e.g. aluminum foil, pennies, etc) you will usually want a smaller lens and 50W or higher power.

The difference in power is important when trying to do deep engraving, coins, cutting very thin metals, etc. It is also important as lenses get wider. Essentially you need much more power if you want to go wider, so if you want to engrave a 300mm wide object, it will require way more power than if you are trying to do a 150mm object.

What upgrade options are there for the 30W and 50W models?

Upgrade options for the 30W and 50W are the Integrated Exhaust Fan, Ring Task Light and Rotary Devices.

Can I get the Integrated Exhaust Fan and Ring Task Light?

Yes. The Integrated Exhaust Fan and Ring Task Light can be purchased separately and added to any EMP fiber laser.

Does my EMP fiber laser come with an LED logo and Smart Keys?

The 60W JPT MOPA and 100W JPT MOPA models include the LED logo and Smart Keys. The 30W and 50W models do not.

Does the LED logo have a function or is it just decoration?

The LED logo is both aesthetically pleasing and shows the status of the machine. It turns red when operating and white when on standby.

What are Smart Keys?

Smart Keys are basically shortcuts mounted on the machine itself. They allow you to Frame, Start and Stop a job. They also serve as the on/off buttons for the exhaust fan and task light IF you get the MOPA models. The Raycus models will not have any of these features, but you will be able to add the fan and light which will then have on/off buttons installed on the cables themselves, similar to a desk lamp.

What rotaries is the EMP Fiber Laser compatible with?

EMP Fiber lasers are compatible with the following Aeon compatible rotaries: PiBurn, RotoBoss, Rotary Chuck, and Smart Roller Rotary. It is not compatible with the Multi Roller.

How does laser output power affect productivity and ROI?

Because a galvo laser can move so quickly, the ability to have more power is usually a nice thing to have as we can go faster for the given job and still deliver the same engrave. Galvos go over the same area over and over again to produce the job or cut desired, so having the increase in power shortens the timeframe required to complete the task.

Whether or not the wattage will perform to your liking, largely depends on your application and desired cycle times. The most common reason for increasing the wattage is to perform deep engraves in less time.

Which fiber laser model is best for annealing steel?

Annealing steels produce a black color on the surface and is much easier to do on a JPT source unit.

Can you get colors with EMP fiber lasers?

A standard fiber laser can not achieve colors. However, MOPA lasers can be used for creating color markings on metals. By adjusting pulse parameters, including pulse duration, MOPA lasers can generate a wider range of colors compared to traditional fiber lasers.

Can you extend the laser area more than 8" x 8"? Do you lose quality?

Yes, you can increase the field size by opting for a taller vertical column and a lens with a larger field size. It ends up being roughly a 12" x 12" work area, and while not as powerful of a beam, still produces excellent results.
