AEON is Innovation

For most people, a laser becomes the epicenter of their business. That’s why we’re always looking for ways to enhance our lasers, even long after you’ve made your purchase.

Wiring

US designed and engineered wiring harnesses reduce quality issues, increase safety and ease of end user repair.

Smart Air

Toggles between low and high pressure air seamlessly to increase efficiency and reduce the risk of flare ups.

Enhanced Cutting

Converts any MIRA into a ninja with an upgraded lens and nozzle extendor, for faster and thicker cuts.

Zone Control

Our in-house, U.S. made Iris system isolates fume extraction to reduce beam reflection and material charring, for faster cuts and cleaner engraves.

Multi-Roller

Our in-house, U.S. made multi-roller eliminates having to hover over your laser by batching out tumblers in bulk, instead of one-by-one.
